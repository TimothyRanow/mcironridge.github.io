A message from the founders of IronRidge


We created IronRidge in our free time between work, college, and parenting because we love the Minecraft community. IronRidge was never intended to be a source of income, and we will never operate IronRidge as a for-profit server accordingly. All donations go directly to our monthly and yearly server-related expenses. As for all of the time we put into building this website from scratch, managing the server, and curating our fantastic community - that's on us.
