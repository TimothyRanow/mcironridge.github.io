User Groups


Administrator


  • Prefix: [Admin]
  • Commands: All
  • How to Join: Build IronRidge from Scratch

Moderator


  • Prefix: [Mod]
  • Commands: /kick, /ban, /tempban, /mute, /ignoreclaims
  • Job: Maintain peace inside the server. This includes taking action against rule violators.
  • How to Join: Invitation Only

Pro-Builder


  • Prefix: [Builder]
  • Commands: /mail, /depth, /getpos
  • Job: Design and build server structures with provided materials before the given deadline.
  • How to Join: Complete the Builder Application

Donor (Iron)


  • Prefix: [Donor]
  • How to Join: Support us on Patreon

Donor (Gold)


  • Prefix: [Donor]
  • Commands: /hat
  • How to Join: Support us on Patreon

Donor (Diamond)


  • Prefix: [Donor]
  • Commands: /hat, access to pets
  • How to Join: Support us on Patreon



Plugins


Discord Response


  • Enables /discord command.

Dynmap


  • Powers the Dynmap.

EssentialsX, EssentialsXChat, EssentialsXSpawn


  • Handles most internal server functions, including chat prefixes, message of the day, spawn routing, and more.

GriefPrevention


  • Our anti-grief plugin.

ModifyWorld


  • Assigns permissions to basic Minecraft functions (block breaking, chat), allowing us to power the Greylist.

PermissionsEX


  • Handles user groups and powers the Greylist.

SaneEconomyCore


  • Our lightweight economy plugin.

ShopChest


  • The engine for our hologram-based shop chests.

Vault


  • Requirement for the economy plugin and some EssentialsX functions.

Votifier


  • Not in use.

WorldBorder


  • Stifles the adventurers.

WorldEdit


  • Primarily used to fix improper terrain generation.

WorldGuard


  • Prevents mob griefing and fire spread, among other things.
